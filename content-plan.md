## Content Strategy Notes

- **Days 1, 3, 12, 15** directly showcase spectr-ai or AbiLens
- **Days 2, 4, 5, 7, 10** are reusable tutorials that attract search traffic
- **Days 8, 11, 14** bring in beginners and non-technical readers
- **Day 12** has viral potential (famous hacks + AI = clicks)
- **Day 15** wraps up as a retrospective, good for engagement

## Cover Image Style

## Cross-Post Strategy

1. Publish on dev.to first (canonical URL)
2. Cross-post to Hashnode with `originalArticleURL`
3. Share on Twitter/LinkedIn with key takeaway + screenshot
